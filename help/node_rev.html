Help

With Reviews options you are be able to draw and deposit a review and also know who has a certain review.

  To look for a review:

  • Select the option Reviews list.
  To draw a review:
  • Select the option Draw a review.
  • Fill in the menu in all its parts fingering name and surname of user carefully, otherwise it signals Insert a new user (From 1st Nov. 1996 onwards he/she must be inserted if it is the first time he/she draw a book or a reviews).
  • Insert the password="xxxxxxx".
  • Push "Draw review".
  To deposit a review:
  • Select the option Deposit a review.
  • Fill in the menu in all its parts
  • Insert tye password="xxxxxxx"
  • Push "Deposit review"
  To insert a new user:
  • Select the link Insert a new user.
  • Fill in the menu in all its parts fingering name and surname carefully (It is advisable to put the initials in capital letters and the others in small letters).
  • Insert the password="********".
  • Push "Insert".
  • Control if the insertion with the option User list order by surname has occurred.
  To know who has a certain review:
  • Select the option Who has a certain review?.
  To know the place of reviews:
  • Select the option Book position.
  • Four images will be visualized: the 1st is the map of the library, the 2nd visualizes the shelves of the locker in the corridor, the 3rd the shelves in the eastern wall and the 4th the shelves in the wester wall.
